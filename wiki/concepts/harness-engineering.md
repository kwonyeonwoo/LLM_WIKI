# Harness Engineering

## Definition

Harness engineering is the design of the environment around an AI agent so that work is goal-directed, bounded, verifiable, and recorded. It goes beyond prompt writing by defining contracts, procedures, journals, preferences, tools, and safety rules.

## Good Contract Criteria

- One clear goal.
- Measurable done conditions.
- Specific validation commands or checks.
- One to four done conditions.
- Explicit negative guardrails.
